Operational Variables

Common inquiries regarding budget allocation, targeting parameters, and deployment timelines.

How do you determine the initial budget?

expand_more

We utilize historical account data, industry benchmark CPCs, and keyword search volume to calculate a Minimum Viable Budget (MVB) required to achieve statistical significance within 30 days.

Do you rely solely on Smart Bidding?

expand_more

No. While algorithms are powerful, they require high-quality conversion data. We often begin with manual bidding or portfolio bid strategies to establish a baseline before transitioning to Target CPA or Target ROAS models.

How do you handle B2B targeting precision?

expand_more

We employ aggressive negative keyword lists, audience exclusions, and exact match types to filter out consumer traffic. We also integrate offline conversion tracking to feed lead quality data back into the algorithm.

What is the expected timeframe for ROI improvements?

expand_more

Initial restructuring typically takes 2-4 weeks. Measurable efficiency gains are generally observed between days 45 and 90, as algorithms learn from restructured data inputs and A/B test results mature.

Do you manage ad creative and copy?

expand_more

Yes. We author all ad copy (Headlines, Descriptions, Extensions) adhering to direct-response copywriting principles. We also request and test image/video assets for Performance Max and Display campaigns.

How is reporting structured?

expand_more

Clients receive a live Looker Studio dashboard tracking core KPIs (CPA, ROAS, Impression Share). We conduct formal structural reviews bi-weekly or monthly depending on spend volume.
