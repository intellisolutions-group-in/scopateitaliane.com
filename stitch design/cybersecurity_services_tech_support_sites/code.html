Frequently Asked Questions

What is your SLA for incident response? expand_more
Our SOC provides a 15-minute response time SLA for critical incidents, 24/7/365.
Do you guarantee GDPR compliance? expand_more
Yes. Our data architectures and IAM protocols are mapped directly to GDPR requirements, ensuring full data sovereignty and right-to-erasure compliance.
How often should we conduct penetration testing? expand_more
We recommend comprehensive penetration testing annually, or immediately following any significant structural change to your infrastructure.
Is SOC-as-a-Service necessary if we have an IT team? expand_more
Yes. Standard IT focuses on availability and performance. A SOC focuses exclusively on threat detection, analysis, and active defense, requiring specialized tooling and expertise.
What does 'Zero-Trust' actually mean in practice? expand_more
It means removing implicit trust. Every access request, regardless of origin, is rigorously authenticated, authorized, and continuously validated before access is granted.
